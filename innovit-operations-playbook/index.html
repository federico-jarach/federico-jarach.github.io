INNOVIT Operations Playbook

A Notion-based operating system and single source of truth for a public-private accelerator helping Italian startups enter the US market.

  • 150% increase in cohort capacity
  • 4 to 10 cohorts per year with the same team
  • 370+ sessions organized across 7 cohorts

The Challenge

A small team was operating across two continents with multiple stakeholders, scattered documents, and growing program volume. The accelerator needed a system that could scale program operations without simply adding more headcount.

The System

The playbook centralized program guides, startup tracking, session templates, mentor databases, reporting, and async collaboration. It turned repeated program knowledge into reusable operating infrastructure.

Why It Matters

This is the kind of backend structure that lets ecosystem work scale. Relationship-heavy programs need clear systems, not just charisma and calendar juggling.
