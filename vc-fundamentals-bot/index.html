VC Fundamentals Bot

A public NotebookLM project for querying venture capital concepts, fundraising theory, legal structures, and investment fundamentals.

Why It Exists

Venture capital education can be dense and scattered. The bot was built to make VC Lab curriculum easier to query, connect, and study without rereading hundreds of pages of materials.

What It Demonstrates

The project shows how AI notebooks can become practical internal research tools for founders, operators, investors, and ecosystem teams who need fast access to structured knowledge.

Relevant Skills

  • Founder education
  • Venture capital fundamentals
  • Research workflow acceleration
