Pitch Clarity Coach

A Claude Code skill for early-stage founders who need clarity before they need a deck.

What It Does

Pitch Clarity Coach runs an interview-style coaching session using Guy Kawasaki's 10-slide framework, founder-stage detection, domain overlays, coaching personas, and a skeptical VC due-diligence pass.

Why I Built It

After reviewing hundreds of founder pitches through startup ecosystem work, the recurring problem was not slide design. It was clarity: who the product serves, what pain is urgent, why now, why this team, and what evidence supports the story.

Architecture

  • SKILL.md for orchestration and coaching rules
  • 10-slide question bank based on the Kawasaki structure
  • Stage and domain overlays for more relevant questioning
  • HTML deck template for a clean black-and-white output
